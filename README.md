# FishDecidingComponent

This component will randomly assign the fish a value based on a povided weight of the value. the possible values are Sea Bass, which has a weight of 25 and is the most likely to be picked, salmon with a weight of 15, tuna with a weight of 9, and orca with a weight of 1 and is the least likely to spawn. the fish print their type to the console when they spawn in.
